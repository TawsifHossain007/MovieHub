MovieMaster Pro is a cinematic movie management SPA where users can browse, add, and manage personal movie collections with filters, watchlists, and a dark/light theme.

Features:
- Browse & filter movies by genre & rating
- Add, edit, delete your movies (auth required)
- Top Rated & Recently Added sections on home
- Watchlist & My Collection pages
- Responsive, accessible, and cinematic UI with theme toggle

Tech: React, Vite, Tailwind, Firebase Auth, Node/Express, MongoDB Atlas
